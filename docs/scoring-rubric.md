# Scoring rubric

| Signal | Points | Evidence required |
|---|---:|---|
| Expressed business need | 0-20 | Concrete pain, workflow, or outcome |
| Decision authority | 0-15 | Explicit title or buying influence |
| Urgency | 0-15 | Specific, near-term timeline |
| Budget | 0-15 | Stated and plausible budget |
| Requirements clarity | 0-10 | Scope, volume, systems, or success criteria |
| Potential project value | 0-15 | Business impact, volume, or expansion opportunity |
| AI automation relevance | 0-10 | Direct fit with AI/automation services |

High priority is 75-100, medium is 45-74, and low is 0-44. Missing facts lower the score and become follow-up questions. Demo mode uses a simpler deterministic approximation with the same thresholds.
